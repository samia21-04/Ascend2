Health Tips

💧 Hydration = Performance

Drink 0.5–1 oz per lb of bodyweight daily. Dehydration drops strength by up to 15%.

🌙 Sleep = Gains

7–9 hrs of sleep releases GH for muscle repair. It's when you actually grow.

🥩 Protein Timing

Aim for 20–40g protein within 2 hrs post-workout for optimal muscle protein synthesis.

🧂 Creatine Tip

Take creatine consistently at the same time daily — timing around workouts is fine but consistency matters most.

❤️ Zone 2 for Fat Loss

3–4x/week Zone 2 cardio (conversational pace) trains your body to burn fat as fuel efficiently.
