🦴 HIP FLEXOR CARE
⚠️ Impingement Note: Skip back squats to depth. Use Hack Squat machine, Leg Press, or Smith Machine Squat. Adjust foot placement higher on the platform if hips pinch.
Daily Stretches (even on rest days):
• Hip flexor lunge stretch — 60 sec each side
• 90/90 hip stretch — 60 sec each position
• Pigeon pose — 90 sec each side
• Supine hip rotation — 20 reps each direction

Avoid: Deep back squats, leg raises with anterior tilt, pushing through sharp pinching pain

Do: Glute activation before every session, hip flexor stretching daily, see a trainer if sharp pain persists
❄️ COOL-DOWN (5–10 MIN)
• Seated hamstring stretch — 60 sec each leg
• Standing quad stretch — 45 sec each leg
• Seated glute stretch (figure 4) — 60 sec each side
• Child's pose — 60 sec
• Thoracic rotation — 10 reps each side
• Foam roll: glutes, hamstrings, IT band — 60 sec each
• Deep breathing — 2 min
PROGRESSIVE OVERLOAD GUIDE
Add weight when you complete all reps cleanly for 2 sessions in a row — even 2.5–5 lbs matters over time.

Deload week every 4–6 weeks: cut weight by 40–50%, keep reps. Focus on form and feel.

Rest weeks: Every 8–12 weeks, take a full week off. This is part of the plan, not giving up.
